Mistakes are only for the living.

Human existence is messy. Unless learned, we often don’t know what the right choice is, the right way to act, what to say or how to say it. These instances of error strung together and only sprinkled with intention and right choices is what we call life. Judgement is not fixed and neither are we. That is what truly makes a human.

Miscue is a catalog that aims to challenge others to think about the errors we make in a different perspective and foster learning regarding a multitude of topics to create a better, but appreciative tomorrow.

The projects within this archive conceptually critique, expose, appreciate, or mend human error on a personal, cultural, or systemic level. Have a project that relates to human errors? Send it to us by clicking ‘Add to Cue.’
