Project: Nunchi






I started with an idea of visualizing Nunchi before thinking about incompleteness. I folded Nunchi to make it fit into a container of incompleteness. I really like a word Nunchi, which I never had and pursue to not having it. But on the same time, I always seek Nunchi to become a 人間 (인간, human) not just 人 (인, individual). This makes me doubt irony parts in myself which is related to incompletness of myself. Therefore, it led me to focus on visualizing my defintion of incompleteness and Nunchi. If someone asks about the favourite and the least favourite words in dictionary, I would definetly say Nunchi for both.


You have to seek Nunchi because you cannot see the world with others’ perspectives. But at the same time, you don’t want to seek Nunchi and seek complete freedom which is quite imagery like infinity. I don’t mean to pursuade someone. Every person has their own defintion. I am just showing my deifintion through some visualizaiton and experience. I want to end this text with one sentence which is quite hard to be written in English, so I am going to write it in Korean. 눈치가 없지만 눈치를 안보는걸 추구하는 사람이 눈치에 대해서 말하는건, 눈치가 없어서 일까 아니면 눈치를 보기에 하는것일까. By entering this space, the audience will receive, get, and seek nunchi, ultimately confronting their own incompleteness.
